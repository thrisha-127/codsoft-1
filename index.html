COFFEE

Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, derived from the Coffea plant. It contains caffeine, which acts as a stimulant, enhancing alertness and energy levels. Coffee is enjoyed in various forms, such as espresso, cappuccino, and latte, each with unique preparation methods. Rich in antioxidants, it may offer health benefits, including improved cognitive function and reduced risk of certain diseases. Its cultural significance spans the globe, making it a social and daily ritual for millions.

Black Coffee

Black coffee is a beverage made from roasted coffee beans. The beans are ground and soaked in water, which releases their flavor, color, caffeine content, and nutrients.

Breve

Breve is derived from the Italian word for "short," don't be misled into thinking it is limited to a small cappuccino cup. Each coffee shop boasts its unique rendition of the Breve, some opting for a smooth, velvety foam akin to a latte.

Irish

Irish is a caffeinated alcoholic drink consisting of Irish whiskey, hot coffee and sugar, which has been stirred and topped with cream . The coffee is drunk through the cream.

Dalgona

Dalgona coffee, also known as hand beaten coffee, is a beverage originating from Macau made by whipping equal parts instant coffee powder, sugar, and hot water until it becomes creamy and then adding it to cold or hot milk.

Macchiatos

In Italian, the term "macchiato" translates as "marked" or "stained", meaning a stained or marked coffee. The macchiato is an espresso coffee drink, topped with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk to allow the taste of the espresso to still shine through.

Americano

An americano is quite simply just hot water and espresso. It’ll either be served 1/2 and 1/2 or 1/3 espresso to 2/3 water, depending on the coffee shop in question or how you’ve chosen to brew it.
